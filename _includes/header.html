Handel's Messiah

The 57th Annual Chapel Hill Open Sing

In this beloved Chapel Hill tradition, everyone participates! A guest conductor will direct a community orchestra, vocal soloists, and you – the audience – as the choir! Singers of all experience are welcome!

Saturday, December 13, 2025 at 7:00 PM at the Chapel Hill Bible Church (260 Erwin Road)


The event is free. No rehearsal is required.
Scores will be provided, or bring your own if you have one.
