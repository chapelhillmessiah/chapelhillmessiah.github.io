History of the Messiah Sing

The Annual Open Sing of Handel's Messiah has become a favorite Chapel Hill Christmas tradition. The event was started in 1966 by five newcomers to Chapel Hill: Fred Brooks and his wife Nancy, Mary Frances Boyce, Stan Mattson, and Caryl Thorn.
